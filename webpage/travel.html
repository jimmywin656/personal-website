Vietnam

I went to Vietnam back in middle school with my family. It was a vacation for me and my sisters and for my parents, it was the first time they went back since migrating to the US. We stayed there for a whole month and I got to experience all sorts of Vietnamese culture and meet a lot of my extended family. It was a super fun experience and I got to eat lots of delicious food.
