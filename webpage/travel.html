Vietnam

I went to Vietnam back in middle school with my family. It was a vacation for me and my sisters and for my parents, it was the first time they went back since migrating to the US. We stayed there for a whole month and I got to experience all sorts of Vietnamese culture and meet a lot of my extended family. It was a super fun experience and I got to eat lots of delicious food.

Japan

One of the countries I want to visit the most is Japan. From what I've seen, the country looks beautiful and well organized. I want to experience things such as their convenience stores, public bath houses, and their food. I also really want to experience the cherry blossoms over there. I've seen images of them when they bloom and it's something I want to experience in person.
